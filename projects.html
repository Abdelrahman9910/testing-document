Museum of Leader Gamal Abdel Nasser
Restoration and development of the personal home of leader Gamal
Abdel Nasser to be a national museum that displays his personal and
political life through documentaries and exhibitions of his possessions.
Assignment Authority : Ministry of Culture
Nature of work : development and renewal
Project duration : 34 months
Position : Heliopolis, Cairo
Area : 12,000 square meters
